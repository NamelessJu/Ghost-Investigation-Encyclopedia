Ghost Investigation Encyclopedia

About

Ghost Investigation Encyclopedia is an encyclopedia containing all knowledge about investigating paranormal entities.

It expands and improves on the "Ghost Huntin' - A Survivors Guide" journal commonly used by investigators.

Version

When new updates for Phasmophobia get released, parts of this encyclopedia may become outdated.

This encyclopedia is currently up-to-date with game version 0.7.1.4

Evidences

Evidences are the main way of indentifying ghost types.
All types usually show a unique combination of 3 signs that tell what ghost you're dealing with.

While it is possible to identify a lot of ghost types using their specific behaviour, abilities or other traits, using evidence is by far the easiest and most reliable form of identification and should be used if possible.

Spirit

Spirits are basic ghosts with nearly no special traits or behaviour. They are very powerful, but passive, only attacking when they need to. They defend their place of death to the utmost degree, killing anyone that is caught overstaying their welcome.

Weakness: Spirits are weakened for twice as long (120 seconds) when burning Smudge Sticks near them.

Evidence

  • EMF 5
  • Spirit Box Response
  • Ghost Writing

Ability

None

Evidenceless detection

Smudge the ghost and time how long it takes for it to hunt again. If it doesn't begin a hunt before 120 seconds have passed, it's likely a Spirit.

This test should be done more than once, since other ghosts could also wait a while before hunting after recovering from being weakened by a Smudge Stick.
