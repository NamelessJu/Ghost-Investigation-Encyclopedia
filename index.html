Ghosts

Ghosts are entities formed from violent or sudden deaths, and can haunt locations for up to 1000 years.

They feed on people's fear.

Hunts

Ghosts start to hunt down people when they get scared enough (<= 50% average sanity for most ghost types).

Hunts can be recognized by the front door locking and electrical devices malfunctioning.

The ghost will not immediately be able to manifest when a hunt starts, giving investigators a few seconds to get away.

Once a hunt ends all lights in the location will turn off. Furthermore, you can check if the ghost is still hunting by trying to flip a light switch - if you can't, there's an ongoing hunt.

Cursed Hunts

When cursed possessions trigger a hunt they simultaneously curse them, making all hunts last longer for the remainder of the investigation.

Survival

To survive a hunt you must turn off all electronic equipment, since ghosts can sense them and will know where you are!

Then you can go into a hiding spot, which you should search for and memorize before the ghost starts hunting.
You should try to break the ghost's line of sight to you at least twice before entering your hiding spot, as ghosts check your last known location. They might also check the last hiding spot they have found you at in a previous hunt!

If you are in a dead end, you can escape by smudging the ghost, making it lose it's orientation for 6 seconds and wander around aimlessly.

Ghost Events

Ghost events are non-fatal manifestations ghosts use to scare their victims. If the ghost touches you during such an event, it will hiss at you, reducing your sanity.

These events appear very similar to hunts, yet there are some slight differences you can use to determine which manifestation is happening:

  • Ghosts can immediately start moving during ghost events
  • No ghost flickering during a ghost event (devices still malfunction)
  • Ghosts always make the same sound during their hunts, but varying ones in events
  • Certain sounds are only used for hunts, while some others only get used during events

Known ghost events are:

  • Standing
  • Walking towards someone
  • Air ball floating towards someone
  • Standing with red light

Spirit

Spirits are basic ghosts with nearly no special traits or behaviour. They are very powerful, but passive, only attacking when they need to. They defend their place of death to the utmost degree, killing anyone that is caught overstaying their welcome.

Weakness: Spirits are weakened for twice as long (120 seconds) when burning Smudge Sticks near them.

Evidence

  • EMF Level 5
  • Spirit Box Response
  • Ghost Writing

Ability

None

Evidenceless identification

Smudge the ghost and time how long it takes for it to hunt again. If it doesn't begin a hunt before 120 seconds have passed, it's likely a Spirit.

This test should be done more than once, since other ghosts could also wait a while before hunting after recovering from being weakened by a Smudge Stick.

Wraith

Wraiths are one of the most dangerous ghosts you will find. It is also the only known ghost that has the ability of flight and has sometimes been known to travel through walls.

Strength: Wraiths almost never touch the ground, meaning it can't be tracked by footsteps.

Weakness: Wraiths have a toxic reaction to Salt.

Evidence

  • EMF Level 5
  • Spirit Box Response
  • D.O.T.S Silhouette

Ability

Can go through walls (?)

Evidenceless identification

Wraiths can easily be identified by their missing footsteps after walking through Salt.

Beware: The Salt will still have a footprint in it!

Phantom

A Phantom is a ghost that can possess the living, inducing fear into those around it. They are most commonly summoned from Ouija Boards.

Strength: Looking at a Phantom will drop your sanity considerably faster.

Weakness: Taking a photo of the Phantom will make it temporarily disappear.

Evidence

  • Spirit Box Response
  • Fingerprints
  • D.O.T.S Silhouette

Ability

?

Evidenceless identification

The safest and easiest option for checking for Phantoms is to take a photo of the ghost outside of a hunt. If it turns invisible while still remaining manifested and the photo doesn't show it, you were looking at a Phantom.

Another sign for a Phantom is their shorter visibility during hunts.

Phantoms will manifest as shadows more often than other ghosts.

Poltergeist

One of the most famous ghosts, the Poltergeist. Known to manipulate objects around it to spread fear into it's victims.

Strength: Poltergeists can throw multiple objects at once, and with great force.

Weakness: With nothing to throw, Poltergeists become powerless.

Evidence

  • Spirit Box Response
  • Fingerprints
  • Ghost Writing

Ability

Throwing several objects at once

Evidenceless identification

Poltergeists consistently throw an object every 0.5 seconds during hunts, which is makes the easiest yet dangerous Phantom test.

Otherwise, you could make a pile of objects to try to provoke the Poltergeist's multithrow ability.

Banshee

The singing siren, known for attracting its victims through song. It has been known to single out its prey before making a killing blow.

Strength: Banshees will weaken their target before striking.

Weakness: Banshees can sometimes be heard screaming with a parabolic microphone.

Evidence

Evidences are the main way of indentifying ghost types.
All ghosts usually show a specific combination of 3 signs that tell what type you're dealing with.

While it is possible to identify a lot of ghost types using their specific behaviour, abilities or other traits, using evidence is by far the easiest and most reliable form of identification and should be used when possible.

Evidence Types

  • Freezing Temperatures
  • EMF Level 5
  • Spirit Box Response
  • Fingerprints
  • Ghost Writing
  • Ghost Orbs
  • D.O.T.S Silhouette

Freezing Temperatures

Some ghosts cool down the area around them more than others, bringing the temperature down to freezing values.

These are detectable either by checking with a Thermometer or by watching your breath: in freezing temperatures your breath will become visible.

Freezing breath

EMF Level 5

All ghosts leave behind electromagnetic fields (short: EMF) when interacting with objects and at places they manifest at, however only certain ghosts can reach an EMF strength of level 5.

You can measure the EMF strength of objects a ghost recently interacted with using an EMF Reader, or alternatively by watching your activity chart in the truck: if you see a spike of 5, it's possibly an EMF level 5 interaction.

Ghosts will produce fields of strength 5 with about every other interaction on average - provided they're able to.

Spirit Box Response

Ghost types with spirit box responses as an evidence can respond when talking to them on a spirit box.

Questions that have been reported to get responses are: asking the ghost about it's location, age, and whether it's friendly - however you are only able to get a reaction if the ghost is nearby and the light in the room is turned off.

If the ghost responds, the ghost symbol on the spirit box will light up while the response is being played.

Spirit box playing a ghost's response

Fingerprints

Specific ghosts leave behind hand- and fingerprints on certain objects after touching them.

These are not visible with the naked eye, but ultraviolet light from UV flashlights or glowsticks will reveal them.

Some object types that ghosts will leave prints on are:

  • Doors
  • Light switches
  • Cooler boxes
  • Keyboards

Ghost Writing

Some ghosts are able to use a pen to write or draw something.

Place a book with a pen in an area where the ghost often walks through and wait for the ghost to write in it.

The longer the ghost doesn't use the book, the less likely it is that it is able to do so.
If the book is thrown by the ghost without it being written or drawn in, you can rule out ghost writing - ghosts always prefer to write instead of throwing the book if they're able to.

Book with writing left by a ghost

Ghost Orbs

Ghost orbs form in the favourite room of some ghost types.

They appear as small balls of light randomly floating around for a short while before disappearing again.
To be able to spot them you should use a video camera with night vision and extinguish all lights in the room.

There have been reports of ghost orb sightings without the use of night vision, however these are very rare occurances.

A ghost orb seen through a night vision camera

D.O.T.S Silhouette

Ghosts with this evidence can be made visible with a D.O.T.S sensor.

Place a D.O.T.S sensor on the ground to cover the largest possible area with it and watch it for a silhouette quickly running through the area of the projected light dots.

It is possible to see these silhouettes with the naked eye, but it is easier to spot them through night vision.
