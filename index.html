About

Evidences

Evidences are the main way of indentifying ghost types.
All ghosts usually show a specific combination of 3 signs that tell what type you're dealing with.

While it is possible to identify a lot of ghost types using their specific behaviour, abilities or other traits, using evidence is by far the easiest and most reliable form of identification and should be used when possible.

Ghost Huntin' Distribution provides all investigators with one of each required evidence tool for free on every investigation to help beginners start out their ghost hunting carreer and prevent bankruptcy.

Spirit

Spirits are basic ghosts with nearly no special traits or behaviour. They are very powerful, but passive, only attacking when they need to. They defend their place of death to the utmost degree, killing anyone that is caught overstaying their welcome.

Weakness: Spirits are weakened for twice as long (120 seconds) when burning Smudge Sticks near them.

Evidence

  • EMF 5
  • Spirit Box Response
  • Ghost Writing

Ability

None

Evidenceless detection

Smudge the ghost and time how long it takes for it to hunt again. If it doesn't begin a hunt before 120 seconds have passed, it's likely a Spirit.

This test should be done more than once, since other ghosts could also wait a while before hunting after recovering from being weakened by a Smudge Stick.
