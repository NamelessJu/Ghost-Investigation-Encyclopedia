About

Ghost Investigation Encyclopedia is a book containing everything you need to know about investigating ghosts.

It is an in-depth version of the "Ghost Huntin' - A Survivors Guide" journal commonly used by paranormal investigators.

Ghosts

Ghosts are entities formed from violent or sudden deaths, and can haunt locations for up to 1000 years.
They get summoned to the realm of the living by careless use of cursed objects.

All ghosts cool down the area around them, allowing to roughly locate them using a thermometer. This requires the location's power source to be turned on for rooms to heat up.

Most ghost types have unique abilites, which they can use at random or in specific circumstances, depending on the individual ability. Most abilities can only be used outside of hunts.

Favourite Room

Ghosts mainly haunt one specific room, but may still wander away from it.
While investigating ghosts it is useful to locate the ghost's favourite room for evidence collection.

On larger locations one should first narrow down the search area using a parabolic microphone.

In smaller areas you can walk around with an EMF reader and listen for interactions - as soon as you hear something, search the object by scanning for EMF in the direction you heard the sound from.
The ghost's favourite room should be very close to where the interaction occured.

As mentioned on the previous page, it is also possible to detect the room by measuring temperatures.

Hunts

Ghosts gain power from fear. Most ghost types reach enough power for an attack at an average sanity of 50%.

When a hunt starts, the front door locks and electrical devices around the ghost start malfunctioning.

It takes ghosts a moment to build up enough power for deadly manifestations, giving investigators a few seconds to get away.
After that, they accelerate when they have a target in sight.

Once the ghost calms down, it gets sent back to it's room and all lights in the location are turned off, after the switches are blocked during the hunt.

Cursed Hunts

Whenever cursed possessions cause a hunt they simultaneously curse them, making that and any future hunt last longer.

Survival

The first step to be able to survive a hunting ghost is to search and memorize a hiding spot before you get hunted!

Ghosts can sense activated electrical equipment around them and will be notified of your location if you are holding one.

Try to break the ghost's line of sight to you before entering a hiding spot, as ghosts check your last known location! They may also search the last hiding spot they have found you at in a previous hunt!

Preferable hiding spots are lockers and cabinets, which you need to hold closed!

You can escape the ghost by smudging it, which disorients it for 6 seconds, causing it to wander around aimlessly.

Ghost Events

Ghost events are non-fatal manifestations ghosts use to scare their victims. If the ghost touches you during such an event, it will hiss at you, reducing your sanity.

These events appear very similar to hunts, yet there are some slight differences you can use to determine which manifestation is happening:

  • Ghosts can immediately start moving during ghost events
  • No ghost flickering during a ghost event (devices still malfunction)
  • Ghosts always make the same sound during their hunts, but varying ones in events
  • Certain sounds are only used in hunts, while some others are only made during events

Known ghost events are:

  • Standing
  • Walking towards someone
  • Air ball floating towards someone
  • Standing with red light

Poltergeist

Evidence

Spirit Box Response Fingerprints Ghost Writing

Behaviour

Poltergeists, as their name suggests, love to throw objects, which they can do with much greater force than other ghosts.
If they have nothing to throw, they become powerless.

While generally throwing things more often, Poltergeists are especially noticeable during hunts, where they consistently throw an item every half a second.

Ability

Throwing multiple items at once, reducing sanity by 2% per thrown object

Hunting threshold

50% average sanity

Speed

Base: 1.7 m/s
Maximum: 2.805 m/s

Banshee

Evidence

Fingerprints Ghost Orbs D.O.T.S Silhouette

Behaviour

Banshees pick a target, only attacking that person until they die, while ignoring everyone else. Once the current target dies, a new one gets picked.

If their target is outside of the location, Banshees will kill anyone! Note that the target's sanity still determines their ability to hunt in this scenario!

Banshee scream

Banshees are singing sirens, only rarely making any other sound during manifestations. They also have a unique scream that can be occasionally heard with a parabolic microphone.

Ability

Wandering to their target to stalk them

Hunting threshold

50% target sanity

Speed

Base: 1.7 m/s
Maximum: 2.805 m/s

Jinn

Evidence

EMF Level 5 Fingerprints Freezing Temperatures

Behaviour

Jinns can use the location's power to approach people to a distance of 3 meters with greater speed during hunts. This can be prevented by turning off the power source.

Since their strengths heavily rely on using the electricity of the location, Jinns will never turn the power off on their own - turning on too many lights can still blow the fuse however!

Ability

Draining 25% sanity in an instant when near someone, producing an EMF at the power source

only if the power is turned on

Hunting threshold

50% average sanity

Evidence

Evidences are the main way of indentifying ghost types.
All ghosts usually show a specific combination of 3 evidences that tell what type you're dealing with.

While it is possible to identify a lot of ghost types using their specific behaviour, abilities or other traits, using evidence is by far the easiest and most reliable form of identification and should be used when possible.

Evidence Types

  • Freezing Temperatures
  • EMF Level 5
  • Spirit Box Response
  • Fingerprints
  • Ghost Writing
  • Ghost Orbs
  • D.O.T.S Silhouette

Freezing Temperatures

Some ghosts cool down the area around them more than others, bringing the temperature down to freezing values.

These are detectable either by checking with a thermometer or by watching your breath: in freezing temperatures your breath will become visible.

Freezing breath

EMF Level 5

All ghosts leave behind electromagnetic fields (short: EMF) when interacting with objects and at places they manifest at, however only certain ghosts can reach an EMF strength of level 5.

You can measure the EMF strength of objects a ghost recently interacted with using an EMF Reader, or alternatively by watching your activity chart in the truck if you see a spike of 5, it's possibly an EMF level 5 interaction.

Ghosts will produce fields of strength 5 with about every other interaction on average - provided they're able to.

EMF Reader detecting an EMF of level 5
Activity spike of 5

Spirit Box Response

Ghost types with spirit box responses as an evidence can respond when talking to them on a spirit box.

Questions that have been reported to get responses are: asking the ghost about it's location, age, and whether it's friendly - however you are only able to get a reaction if the ghost is nearby and the light in the room is turned off.

If the ghost responds, the ghost symbol on the spirit box will light up to indicate that a response is being received.

Spirit box playing a ghost's response

Fingerprints

Specific ghosts leave behind hand- and fingerprints on certain objects after touching them.

These are not visible on themselves, but ultraviolet light from UV flashlights or glowsticks can reveal them.

Some types of objects that ghosts have been reported to leave prints on are:

  • Doors
  • Light switches
  • Windows
  • Cooler boxes
  • Keyboards

Ghost Writing

Some ghosts are able to use a pen to write or draw something.

Place a book with a pen in an area where the ghost often walks through and wait for the ghost to write in it.

The longer the ghost doesn't use the book, the less likely it is that it is able to do so.
If the book is thrown by the ghost without it being written or drawn in, you can rule out ghost writing - ghosts always prefer to write instead of throwing the book if they're able to.

Ghost Orbs

Ghost orbs form in the favourite room of some ghost types.

They appear as small balls of light randomly floating around for a short while before disappearing again.
To be able to spot them you should use a video camera with night vision and extinguish all lights in the room.

There have been reports of ghost orb sightings without the use of night vision, however this only seems to be possible in very specific lighting conditions, meaning that these are very rare occurances.

A ghost orb seen through a night vision camera

D.O.T.S Silhouette

Ghosts with this evidence can be made visible with a D.O.T.S projector.

Place such a sensor on the ground to cover the largest possible area with it and watch it for a silhouette quickly running through the area of the projected light dots.

It is possible to see these silhouettes with the naked eye, but it is easier to spot them through night vision.
